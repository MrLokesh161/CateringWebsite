About Us

Cooking Since 2009

Welcome to Sai Aksharaiya Foods! We're all about making delicious vegetarian food. We started our journey in 2009 in Coimbatore. Our founder, Srinivasan Iyengar, had a passion for cooking and wanted to share his love for food with everyone.

Srinivasan Iyengar laid the foundation of our restaurant with his dedication and hard work. Although he's no longer with us, his legacy lives on through his son, Sathish Kumar, who continues his father's mission of serving scrumptious food. Sathish Kumar is joined by Mr. Sathya Narayanan, together bringing over 60 years of combined experience to the table.

Our chefs are really good at what they do. They use fresh ingredients and follow strict cleanliness rules to make sure everything is top-notch. We offer a variety of cuisines, from traditional to modern, so there's something for everyone.

Whether it's a wedding, a business event, or just a family get-together, we're here to make it special. Our menu has everything from spicy Chettinad dishes to tasty North Indian treats.
